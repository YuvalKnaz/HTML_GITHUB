Classic Moments

Founded as Newton Heath in 1878, the club became Manchester United in 1902. The Busby Babes era of the 1950s and Sir Alex Ferguson's dominance from the 1990s to 2013 are the two legendary pillars in the club's history.

Major honors include 20 Premier League titles, 12 FA Cups, 3 European Cups/Champions League titles, and 1 Club World Cup.
